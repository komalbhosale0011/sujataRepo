MCA Department




MCA department started in June 2006 with the aim of providing quality education in computer applications to the students with modern infrastructure and competent faculty members.

Department is functional under Jayawantrao Sawant College of Engineering, Hadapsar, it provides MCA degree and is affiliated to University of Pune.
