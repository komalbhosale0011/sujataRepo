Jayawantrao Sawant College of Engineering (JSCOE) is approved by the All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE),

New Delhi and Govt. Of Maharashtra affiliated to Pune University.

It was established by JSPM on the second campus at Hadapsar.

The College location has a history as pilgrims traveling from dehu and Alandi to Pandharpur used to rest here.

It was called as visawa. We now say that students would come here for four years to study and then leave the campus after acquiring knowledge.
